Ju Jingyi

Ju Jingyi, a popular mainland actress and singer, was born on June 18, 1994. She is skilled in singing, dancing, violin, piano, and more. Debuted on November 2, 2013, she won first place in the SNH48 general election for two consecutive years and was promoted to the Hall of Fame.

On December 15, 2017, she established her own studio and pursued a solo career. On June 18, 2024, Ju Jingyi's contract with her former company expired and was not renewed. At thirty, one is established and ready to embark on new journey. In the future, she will continue to cultivate her beloved acting career. May you and her walk side by side, hand in hand, on this journey.
