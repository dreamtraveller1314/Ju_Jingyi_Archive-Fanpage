# Ju Jingyi Archive - Fanpage
This is a fan-made website to introduce the Chinese actress and singer, Ju Jingyi. Ju Jingyi, a popular China mainland actress and singer, was born on June 18, 1994. She is skilled in singing, dancing, violin, piano, and more. Debuted on November 2, 2013, she won first place in the SNH48 general election for two consecutive years and was promoted to the Hall of Fame.
